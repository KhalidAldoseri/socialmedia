Types of Social Media

Different types of social media networks are following :

  1. Social Networks : Facebook , Twitter , Linkedin.
  2. Media sharing networks : Instagram , Snapchat , YouTube
  3. Discussion forums : Reddit , Quora , Digg
  4. Bookmarking & Content curation networks : Pinterest , Flipboard
  5. Consumer review networks : Yelp , Zomato , TripAdvisor
  6. Blogging & Publishing networks : Wordpress , Tumblr , Medium
  7. Social shopping networks : Polyvore , Etsy , Fancy
  8. Interest based networks : Goodreads , Houzz , LastFm.

Purpose of Social Media

  • News Consumption
  • News Production
  • News Dissemination

News Consumption

This is used for common purpose for most of the people . Because this is shown passive behaviour. News consumption is nothing but the reading of articles or watching videos/pictures.

News Production

This is shown passive behaviour. News production is a good habbit as it is includes writing articles and participating in discussions .

News Dissemination

It implies sharing and forwarding news articles , links or videos/ Pictures.
