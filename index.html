History of Social Media

The ARPANET (Advanced Research Projects Agency Network) emerge this idea in 1969 . This early Digital network created by the United States Department of Defence . They experiments this model at four universities start sharing software , hardware and other data.

Tn 1987 , National Science Foundation launched a more robust , secure and nationwide digital network known as NSFNET . That was the direct precursor to today's internet.

In 1997 , After a decade the first true social media platform was launched.
